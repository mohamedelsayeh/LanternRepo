Kasa Account
 
While you don't have to have Kasa account to use your devices, creating an account added functionality, such as:
  • Synchronization of settings and configurations to all your mobile devices
  • Ability to control and configure devices from outside home
  • Ability to customize your TP-LINK Smart home devices with "Scenes" for a truly automated experience
  • Use of a third-party services and products such as Amazon Echo

Devices can only be associated with one Kasa account. To remove association, turn off Remote Control in the device's settings, or factory reset your device.

please note that if you already have a TP-LINK Cloud account associated with other TP-LINK app (such as tpCamera), you can use it to sign in to Kasa.
